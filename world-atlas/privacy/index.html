World Atlas Privacy Policy

Clear information about what World Atlas stores, uses, and shares.

Effective date: June 10, 2026

World Atlas is a country information app that lets users browse, compare, save, and share country details.

No account required World Atlas does not require sign in and does not create user accounts.
Local app data Favourites, settings, notes, tags, and cached country data are stored locally on your device.
Public country data Country information is provided by REST Countries API v3.1.

Information Stored On Device

The app stores favourites, app settings, notes, tags, and cached country data locally on your device. This information is used only to provide app features such as favourites, faster loading, theme preferences, language preferences, and cache management.

Data Sources

Country information is provided by REST Countries API v3.1. This includes country names, flags, capitals, population, area, currencies, languages, maps, time zones, calling codes, borders, and related public country data.

Data source: REST Countries

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to show ads. Google and its partners may collect or use device information, advertising identifiers, approximate location, app interactions, diagnostics, and other data for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and compliance.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

Sharing

When users choose to share country information, Android's system share sheet is used. The selected destination app controls how that shared content is handled.
