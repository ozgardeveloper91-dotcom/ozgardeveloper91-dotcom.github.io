World Atlas Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

World Atlas stores favourites, settings, notes, tags, and cached country data locally on your device.

The app uses REST Countries API for public country information.

The app uses Google AdMob to show ads. Google may collect or use device information for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and compliance.

The app does not require sign in and does not create user accounts.
