Privacy Policy for ScannerPDF

ScannerPDF is a document scanning app that helps users capture, crop, enhance, organize, export, print, and share scanned documents. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information the App Uses

ScannerPDF may use the following information or device features:

  • Camera: used to capture photos for scanning.
  • Photos or media selected by you: used when importing images from your gallery.
  • Local files created by the app: used to save recent scans, thumbnails, PDFs, Word documents, and backup files.
  • Text recognition: used to extract text from images when you choose the Text/OCR feature.

How Your Scans Are Stored

Scanned images and recent document records are stored locally on your device inside the app storage. When you export, share, print, save to gallery, save to downloads, or create a backup, the app only uses the files you choose to process.

Advertising

ScannerPDF uses Google AdMob to show ads. Google and its partners may collect or use device information, advertising identifiers, approximate location, app activity, and other data to provide and measure ads, depending on your device settings and consent choices.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Text Recognition

ScannerPDF uses Google ML Kit text recognition to extract text from scanned images. Text recognition is used only when you choose the text extraction feature.

Sharing and Exporting

The app lets you export or share files as images, PDFs, Word documents, printed documents, or backup ZIP files. Once you share or export a file to another app or service, that app or service handles the file under its own privacy policy.

Backup and Restore

Backup files may contain scan titles, folder names, timestamps, and scan images. Keep backup ZIP files private and share them only with services or people you trust.

Children's Privacy

ScannerPDF is not directed to children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

We use reasonable app-level practices to keep scan data local unless you choose to export, share, print, or back it up. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, so users should avoid scanning or sharing sensitive documents unless they are comfortable with the destination app or service.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be identified by a new effective date.
