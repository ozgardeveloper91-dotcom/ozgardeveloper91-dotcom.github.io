Privacy Policy

App: World Football Cup 2026

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Overview

World Football Cup 2026 is a football fixtures, results, teams, groups, and favorites tracker. This app is not affiliated with FIFA or any official football organization.

Information We Collect

We do not require users to create an account and we do not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.

Local Data

The app may store match fixtures, teams, standings, favorite teams, favorite matches, settings, and cached football data locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

The app uses football-data.org to provide football fixtures, results, teams, and standings.

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google AdMob may collect device identifiers, advertising data, diagnostics, and usage information according to Google’s own policies.

Notifications

If you enable match reminders, the app may request notification permission. Notifications are used only to remind you about saved or selected football matches.

Internet Access

The app uses internet access to refresh football data and load advertisements.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data handled by third-party services such as Google AdMob and football-data.org is subject to their respective privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
